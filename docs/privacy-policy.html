Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 10, 2026 • Last Updated: March 10, 2026

5Emojis ("we," "us," or "our") is operated by Desi Digital LLC. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the 5Emojis mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your name, email address, date of birth, and authentication credentials through Apple Sign In or Google Sign In.

Profile Information: You may provide additional details such as photos, emojis, profession, interests, languages, pronouns, and other profile fields you choose to fill out.

Location Data: With your permission, we collect your approximate location to show you people nearby. We do not track your location in the background.

Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with the App, including swipes, matches, and messages, to provide and improve our services.

Device Information: We collect device identifiers and push notification tokens to deliver notifications.

Photos: Photos you upload are stored securely and may be processed for content moderation to ensure community safety.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To show your profile to other users and facilitate connections
  • To provide location-based friend discovery
  • To send push notifications about matches, messages, and app updates
  • To moderate content and enforce our community guidelines
  • To process subscriptions and in-app purchases
  • To improve and maintain the App

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Other Users: Your profile information (name, photos, emojis, age, approximate distance, and other profile fields) is visible to other users of the App.
  • Service Providers: We use third-party services including Supabase (database and authentication), RevenueCat (subscription management), and OpenAI (content moderation). These providers process data on our behalf under contractual obligations.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the safety of our users.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, which provides encryption at rest and in transit. We implement row-level security policies to ensure users can only access data they are authorized to see. While we take reasonable measures to protect your information, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access and Update: You can view and update your profile information at any time within the App.
  • Delete Your Account: You can request account deletion by contacting us. We will delete your profile, photos, matches, and messages.
  • Location: You can disable location permissions in your device settings at any time.
  • Notifications: You can manage push notification preferences in your device settings.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we learn that we have collected information from a user under 18, we will delete that information promptly.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or by other means. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
